Guide What are you breathing?

PM2.5 & PM10

Tiny particles from combustion or dust. They are small enough to enter your lungs and bloodstream.

NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide)

Mostly comes from burning fuel (cars/trucks). It can aggravate asthma and respiratory infections.
